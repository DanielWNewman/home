10 Books you should read today

May 20, 2020 --- Daniel W. Newman

  Here are 10 books you should read ASAP. All of them are available on Gutenberg, so get on over and start reading.

  • War and Peace by Leo Tolstoy
  • Frankenstein; Or, The Modern Prometheus by Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley
  • Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen
  • The Prince of India; Or, Why Constantinople Fell by Lew Wallace(The same Lew who wrote the famed Ben Hur)
  • The Art of War by Sunzi and Wu
  • Utopia by Jane Austen
  • The Hound of the Baskervilles by Arthur Conan Doyle
  • The Cat of Bubastes: A Tale of Ancient Egypt by G. A. Henty
  • The Lion of Saint Mark: A Story of Venice in the Fourteenth Century by G. A. Henty
  • Swiss Family Robinson by Johann David Wyss
